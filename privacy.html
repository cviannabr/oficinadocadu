Privacy Policy — Oficina do Cadu

Last updated: June 7, 2026

"Oficina do Cadu" ("the App") is a personal automation tool operated by an individual hobbyist. It automatically publishes timelapse videos of the operator's own 3D prints to the operator's own social media accounts (such as YouTube and TikTok). This page explains what data the App handles.

1. Who this App is for

The App is built and used by a single person to post their own content to their own accounts. It is not a service offered to other end users and does not have user sign-ups, profiles, or accounts belonging to third parties.

2. Data we collect

The App does not collect, request, or store personal data from any third-party users. It does not use cookies, advertising, tracking pixels, or analytics.

The only credentials handled are the operator's own social-media access tokens (obtained via the standard OAuth login of each platform, e.g. TikTok and YouTube). These tokens are stored locally on the operator's own device and are used solely to upload the operator's own videos to the operator's own accounts. They are never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

3. How we use platform APIs

The App uses the official APIs of the social platforms (for example, the TikTok Content Posting API and the YouTube Data API) only to upload videos that the operator created, to the operator's own account. The App reads no other user's data and posts to no other account.

4. Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The only data leaving the device is the operator's own video file and its title/description, sent to the respective platform's API for publishing, subject to that platform's own privacy policy.

5. Data retention

OAuth tokens are kept locally only for as long as the App is in use and can be revoked at any time by the operator from the platform's settings. Generated videos are stored locally on the operator's own device.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated; the "Last updated" date will reflect any change.

8. Contact
